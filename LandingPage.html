BMTC Bus



Bengaluru Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC), is a state-owned public road transport corporation in the Indian city of Bangalore.You find AC/NON-AC buses to travel in around banglore.
Vajra :It is an AC bus service,serving the tech parks, industrial areas, major bus stations and major residential areas.
Vayu Vajra: It is an AC bus service connecting 20 Metropolitan region to Bangalore's Kempegowda International Airport.
The minimum fare is Rs 10 for the first 2km (stage 1), maximum fare for 50km is only Rs 50 in non AC bus. "Namma BMTC" app has been launched to assist commuters to track buses live, check schedules, plan their journey and calculate fare between stages.
